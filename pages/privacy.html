Privacy Policy



Your privacy is important to you, and as a valued customer that means it is important to us. We are committed to protecting any personal information you entrust to us. This page describes how we use and will not use your personal information and how we use cookies on our website. From time to time we may change this information so please check this page regularly.

What kind of personal information does Deliciousfood.com ask for?

When you make a Reservation with Deliciousfood.com, we will ask for your name, address, email address, telephone number. To make it easier to make and manage reservations, you can create an account so you don’t have to enter these details every time you use Deliciousfood.com.

If you choose to pay online, we will ask for your payment information via our secure online payment, Adyen. We know that your credit/debit card information is very sensitive, so unlike other booking websites, we don’t share it with our Premises partners.

We will also gather information in the background when you visit Deliciousfood.com. This may include your IP address, device type, internet browser, operating system, location and certain settings. All of this is so that we can make sure we’re offering you the best service we can.

What does Deliciousfood.com use your personal information for and who do we share it with?

Reservations & Booking: We use the information you give us to manage your reservations, and to share some of it with the Restauranteurs so that they know who to expect and to serve you the best. In near future we may also share your allergies section; diet if you are following; favorite and wish list to personalize your dining experience.

Customer Service: Giving the best customer service possible is very important to us. We use your information to make sure we serve you as well as we can. If we work with 3rd parties as part of our customer service we may share your information with them (e.g. if you ask us to book transport for you)

Account administration and personalization: We link all of your bookings and reservations to your account, using your information and booking history to allow you to manage your account in the most user-friendly way possible. We may use this information to personalize your account and the way our website looks to you, to ensure it’s as relevant to your needs as possible.

Diners reviews: We’ll use your information to contact you following a Delicious Food, asking you to rate the restauranteurs and our website to help future visitors to Deliciousfood.com.

We appreciate your activities in food community where you tag; associate; rank a food; restaurant; environment; hospitality; taste; specialty; Special Dish that lets the other diners make best choice according to their meeting; mood; company and event. We also display a pic if provided and your name as top Delicious Food of restaurant if applicable.

Marketing activities: We may use your information and reservation history to send you newsletters and special offers relevant to you via email, SMS and push notifications. Of course, you can choose to opt out of these at any time. We may also use this information to show a customized selection of offers and restaurants to you when you use our services. From time to time, we may contact you by phone to suggest offers we think would be of interest to you. We sometimes ask our customers to take part in market research. If you do so, any personal information gathered as part of the research will only be used with your consent.

Other communications: There may be other times when we need to get in touch with you for specific reasons and will use your information in order to do this. Examples of this include: responding to specific requests or direct communications from you; contacting you to finalize, confirm or change a reservation; asking you to review our services or those of our restauranteurs.

Fraud detection and prevention: We are committed to keeping Deliciousfood.com as safe and secure as possible. To help us do that we may use your information for the detection and prevention of fraud, and other unwanted activities.

Improving our services: We constantly analyze the way people use our services to improve our offering and the customer experience. To do this we use your information and data on how you use our services.

How does Deliciousfood.com use social media?

We use social media to promote and improve our services and those of our Restauranteurs. When you click on a social media button on Deliciousfood.com, information is shared with your social media and may be displayed on your profile and or shared with your network.

We may enable you to sign in to Deliciousfood.com using your social media accounts. If you choose to do this your social media will tell you which of your information will be shared with Deliciousfood.com before confirmation.

Deliciousfood.com maintains pages and profiles on several social media platforms. If you connect to Deliciousfood.com the social media service may share your information with Deliciousfood.com. This information helps us to improve our services and tailor them to you.

When does Deliciousfood.com share your data with third parties?

Restauranteurs: We share your relevant personal information (but not your payment card information) with the restaurants so they can know who to expect and prepare as per your reservation. This may include names, reservation type, ordered food, selected buffet, expected time & date; contact details, selected services and other reservation preferences. Wish lists and favorite restaurant lists if created may be shared with highlighted restauranteurs to offer their best tailored offers.

Our parent and sister companies: Deliciousfood.com is part of a larger family of companies all operating under our investors. In order to provide the best possible service to you and customers of our Sister and Parent companies, we may share information between them.

Third party services: We may use third party service s for some parts of our business, in which case we will share your information with them. These may include marketing/advertising companies, outsourced customer care, payments, analytics and consulting companies. These third-parties will be bound by confidentiality agreements and will not be allowed to use your personal information for any purposes other than for the specific work they do for us.

Our business partners and affiliates: Deliciousfood.com works with a network of business partners and affiliates to distribute Restaurants, including but not limited to, restauranteurs, Private dining places, Public Dining Places, Travel Agents, Food and catering services and advertisement companies. In this case we may share some of the information associated with your reservations with these partners so they can manage your reservation and for marketing purposes. In this case the use of your personal information will be subject to the terms and conditions of the business partner in question.

Competent authorities: Where required to do so by law, we will share your personal information with government or investigative authorities (or any regulation having the force of law). Examples of when this might happen include court orders, subpoenas and orders arising from legal processes and criminal investigations. We may also disclose your personal data if it is deemed necessary for the prevention, detection or prosecution of criminal acts.

Security Policy

What security measures does Deliciousfood.com put in place to protect you and your information?

In accordance with data protection laws, we observe reasonable procedures to prevent unauthorized access and the misuse of personal information.

We use appropriate systems and procedures to safeguard the personal information you give us. Our servers are protected by technical and physical restrictions on accessing and using the personal information they contain. Only members of our team who specifically need to use personal information have access to it.

When you pay by credit card, you are redirected to our third party online payment , Adyen. Adyen is an internationally recognized online payments meeting the highest standards of security and stability. Deliciousfood.com does not gather your credit card information.
